Skincare Steps
  1. Wash your face with a cleanser
  2. Take Steam for 3 to 4 minutes
  3. Let your face cool down for a minute or so.
  4. Scrub your face using an exfoliator that suits your skin
  5. Now roll ice all over your face for about 5 minutes to close up your pores and tighten your skin
  6. Let your face air dry
  7. Use a toner
  8. Apply Serum
